Small Business Information

In recent years, students have expressed concern that small and local businesses are being underrepresented at the Career Fair.

Therefore, this year we are offering a reduced fee of $600 to small companies. Criteria for determining what constitutes a small business will be based on the number of employees or on the number of annual dollar revenues, as laid out by the Office of Size Standards of the Small Business Administration.

The decision as to whether your company qualifies as either a small or local business will be made by the Career Fair Executive Committee. Please register as soon as possible, as there are a limited number of booths set aside for companies registering at a reduced rate. Please send any questions or comments to tbp-career-exec.
