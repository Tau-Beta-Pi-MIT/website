COMMUNITY SERVICE

  • A Society Founded Upon Service

    Our members and eligibles are rooted in service, and recognize the value of engaging with the greater Boston community through a variety of different service events. Past service events organized by our members and eligibles have included:

    1. Habitat for Humanity
    2. Girls Who Code
    3. Challah for Hunger
    4. Relay for Life
    5. Boston Red Sox Green Team
    6. Loaves and Fishes Program

    If you have any ideas for service projects or want to get involved with TBP service events, please email us at tbp-officers@mit.edu.
