TUTORING

We strive to provide a supportive academic environment in which students can help each other grow and succeed, and tutoring is another outlet through which we pursue this vision. Our tutoring program matches tutees with tutors based off of their academic experience, and is available free of charge for any course for all undergraduate students.

If you have any questions about our tutoring program or would like to set up tutoring opportunities, please reach out to us at tbp-officers@mit.edu.
