Philanthropic Fellowships


This year, Tau Beta Pi will also be awarding Philanthropic Fellowships, aimed towards recognized MIT student groups that are seeking to increase involvement and raise awareness about their volunteering activities. The sponsored philanthropies may be based in the U.S. or abroad, and there are no restrictions regarding the type of volunteering activities proposed by the student group.

Applications for these fellowships are due on March 16th 2013. This spring, we are seeking to award up to 5 fellowships with up to US $3000 each.

The following documents must be received by March 16, 2013 at 11:59 PM:
  • Cover page consisting of the applicant’s name, contact information, class year, major, and project title.
  • Brief abstract (under 200 words) detailing the project description and objectives.
  • Proposal (under 2000 words) describing community needs, project aims, timeline, technical approaches if applicable, anticipated impacts, and personal motivations/qualifications.
  • Proposed budget, including any other sources of support (applied or confirmed).
  • Letter of recommendation from an MIT faculty member who is familiar with the applicant’s character and project and can attest to his/her integrity and ability.
  • Letter of interest from the organization concerned (if applicable).
  • Resume.

The TBP Philanthropy Fellowship Program has the following requirements:
  • All aspects of the project requiring funding must be completed by the end of Summer 2013
  • Each fellow must submit a 3-5 page written report that includes multiple pictures of his/her project upon completion.
  • Each fellow must be prepared to present his/her accomplishments at the Tau Beta Pi Fellowship Forum (to be announced).
  • Relevant receipts/proofs of payment totaling the awarded sum must be submitted by the end of the summer.
  • TBP reserves the right to reclaim all awarded funds if the above requirements are not fulfilled.

Interviews will be conducted between March 16th - 19th, 2013. Final decisions will be made by March 25th.

All application materials must be submitted electronically to tbp-fellowships@mit.edu

For further information, please contact tbp-fellowships@mit.edu
