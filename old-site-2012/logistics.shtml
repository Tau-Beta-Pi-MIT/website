    Shipping (from MIT)

    We will arrange for a FedEx and UPS truck to be ready near the Career Fair site when the event ends at 3:00pm. Companies are responsible for shipping costs; we request that companies have a pre-paid FedEx label so that packages may be directly loaded into the truck. The career fair staff will take care of packages to be sent via USPS. Please contact the career fair staff if your company plans to ship using a different carrier.

    Resources available to companies

    Fair Booths

    Fair booths are 8' x 10' wide. They are constructed with an 8' backdrop and 3' side rails on an aluminum frame. Each booth will be furnished with one 6' covered and skirted table, two chairs, electrical outlets, and a 2-line identifying sign. Any additional furnishing will be the responsibility of the individual exhibitor.

    Electricity & A/V Equipment

    Tau Beta Pi will provide electricity for all booths. Overhead projectors and TV/VCR combos are available upon request, at no additional charge. LCD projectors are available for a fee of $200. Please note A/V requests on your registration form. Phone lines and/or LAN drops will not be available.

    Information Sessions

    We will do our best to accommodate information session requests on a first-come, first-serve basis. Please indicate your request for an information session on the registration form. The earlier you register, the more likely we will be able to arrange to host an information session.
