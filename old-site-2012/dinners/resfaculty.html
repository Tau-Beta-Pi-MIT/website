New Reservation Policy

Please notify us at least three business days in advance if you need to cancel your reservation (failure to do so may result in the cancellation of future reservations and/or a fine to cover the cost of the dinner). If you submit this form more than once, your new information will replace information from previous submissions. Please indicate that you are resubmitting the form in the Notes section of the form. If you have any questions on the reservation policy or about the Leonardo dinners, please email tbp-leonardo.

We expect that all members attend all dinners for which they have reservations and that they dress appropriately for each dinner (that is in semi-formal business attire). Neglecting to do either of these may result in the cancellation of any future dinner reservations and/or a fine to cover the cost of the dinner.
