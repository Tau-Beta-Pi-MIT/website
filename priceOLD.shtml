    Payment and Pricing

    There are five levels of registration in the career fair:


    Basic Registration: $1400

    • One booth
    • Meals for up to 5 company representatives
    • 3 tickets to the career fair networking reception
    • 1 copy of the MIT TBP resume e-book
    • Company listing in Career Fair Guide and Web Page

    Non-Profits: $300

    • Same services as Basic registration

    Small Business: $600

    • Same services as Basic registration
    • Must meet the Small Business Requirements

    Silver Sponsorship: $2800

    • $1800 services plus $1000 possible tax-deductible donation
    • One booth
    • Preferred booth location
    • Meals for up to 5 company representatives
    • 3 tickets to the career fair networking reception
    • 1 copy of the MIT TBP resume e-book
    • Company listing in Career Fair Guide and Web Page
    • Company logo in Career Fair Guide
    • Company logo on Career Fair Web Page

    Gold Sponsorship: $4200

    • $2200 services plus $2000 possible tax-deductible donation
    • Two adjacent booths
    • Premium booth location
    • Meals for up to 5 company representatives
    • 4 tickets to the career fair networking reception
    • 1 copy of the MIT TBP resume e-book
    • Company listing in Career Fair Guide and Web Page
    • Best placement of large company logo in Career Fair Guide
    • Best placement of large company logo on Career Fair Web Page
    • Company logo in MIT Student Newspaper advertisement
    • Company logo on all Career Fair posters
