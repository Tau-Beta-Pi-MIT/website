Social Events


Tau Beta Pi organizes 4 to 5 free social events each term, which may include dinner outings, movie nights, comedy club trips, ice cream runs, and more! Current members and eligibles are contacted by the Social Chairs through emails when an event is planned. Events will also be posted on the TBP Calendar.
